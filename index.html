Our Product

The product Biscuit-Heart is related to the goal Good Health and well Being. It helps people who suffer from hypertension by adding fiber to them. Our source of fiber are lentils.

About our Product:

We are known for using organic and gluten free ingredients. The ingredients inculded in our muffin are: Lentils, almond flour, egg, oatmeal, vegetable oil, natural dark chocolate, and brown sugar.

Lentils are our main source of Fiber.

Fiber prevents heart disease through to stem from its ability to lower both blood pressure and cholesterol.
